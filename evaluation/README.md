Sohail AI Evaluation Framework
This folder contains the Sohail AI Evaluation Checklist v1.0 — a practical framework for reviewing AI projects and AI services before, during, and after deployment.
It is based on:

NIST AI Risk Management Framework (AI RMF)
ISO/IEC 42001 (AI Management Systems)
UAE AI Governance Principles

Contents

Section 1 — Pre-Development checklist
Section 2 — Development checklist
Section 3 — Pre-Deployment checklist
Section 4 — Post-Deployment checklist
Service-specific adaptations (AI Agents, AI Automation)
Implementation roadmap

Each checklist item documents why it matters, the responsible owner, the required evidence, and a risk level.
